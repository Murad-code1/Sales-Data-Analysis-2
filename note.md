## Business Insights

1. **Top-Selling Categories:**  
   "Food and Beverages" and "Sports and Travel" generate the highest revenue among all product lines.  
   → Focus marketing efforts and inventory on these categories to maximize profitability.

2. **Customer Distribution:**  
   Sales are almost evenly split between Member and Normal customers.  
   → There is a strong opportunity to convert Normal customers into Members through loyalty programs and promotions.

3. **Peak Sales Hours:**  
   Sales peak around 13:00 and 19:00.  
   → Increase staff availability and optimize operations during these hours to improve customer experience and reduce wait times.

4. **Balanced Branch Performance:**  
   Sales across branches (A, B, C) are relatively similar with minimal differences.  
   → Indicates consistent performance, but also suggests room for targeted strategies to differentiate branch performance.
